Kids Zone is a friendly collection of simple, colorful games made just for young children — from toddlers to early grade-schoolers. No clutter, no confusing menus, just big buttons, happy sounds, and games that kids can play on their own.

Every game is designed to be picked up in seconds and enjoyed without reading. Bright visuals, gentle sound effects, and forgiving gameplay keep little ones smiling while they learn and play.

🎮 FOUR GAMES IN ONE APP

🃏 Simple Pairs (ages 2–5)
A classic memory matching game. Flip the cards, find the pairs, and clear the board. Choose from Easy to Expert so the challenge grows with your child. Great for memory, focus, and concentration.

🎨 Color Mixer (ages 4–8)
Mix red, yellow, and blue to discover orange, green, purple, and more! Play freely or take on color challenges, then name and save your own creations. A playful introduction to colors and creativity.

🐭 Mouse Maze (ages 3–8)
Swipe to guide the little mouse through the maze to its cheese. Mazes get trickier as kids go, building problem-solving and logical thinking — with a hint always one tap away.

🏹 Balloon Archer (ages 5–8)
Aim your bow, pull back, and pop the balloons! A gentle archery arcade game that sharpens aim, timing, and hand-eye coordination across fun levels.
