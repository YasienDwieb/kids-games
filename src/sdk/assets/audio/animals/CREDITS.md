## Per-clip notes (duration / processing)

- **lion** — 9.0s, used as-is. Male lion roar recorded in a Tamil Nadu zoo. Source description: "the roaring of a lion in captivity." Same uploader as the elephant clip.
- **elephant** — 1.4s, used as-is. Genuine elephant trumpeting (Tamil caption: யானையின் பிளிறல்).
- **dog** — trimmed to 4.0s from a 47s CC0 recording ("A crying normal puppy").
- **cat** — trimmed to 3.2s (two meows) from an 11.9s PD recording ("Cat asking to get out").
- **frog** — trimmed to 4.0s from a 22s PD recording of grass frog (Rana temporaria) mating calls ("Grasfrosch Paarungsrufe").
- **horse** — 2.3s, used as-is. "A sound recording of a neighing horse."
- **sheep** — trimmed to 2.6s (first strong bleat) from an 8.2s PD recording. Recorded at Maan Farms, Abbotsford, BC. (A shorter 0.48s CC0 "Sheep bleat.ogg" exists but was too quiet; the PD clip is louder/clearer.)
- **rooster** — trimmed to 2.1s (the crow) from a 7.3s PD recording of a bantam Sebright rooster.
- **duck** — 3.7s CC0 recording of ducks quacking ("Ducks snatching in Ravenhof park, Torhout, Belgium"). Quacking confirmed via spectrogram (repeated harmonic bursts).
- **bird** — trimmed to 5.0s from a 209s CC0 garden-birdsong recording.
- **bee** — trimmed to 4.0s from a 10.9s PD recording of a bumblebee buzzing (recorded in Southern Finland).
